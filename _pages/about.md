Long Yuan is currently a Professor at the school of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST). Before joining NJUST, he was a Research Associate in the [Data and Knowledge Research Group](https://unswdb.github.io/) at University of New South Wales (UNSW), Australia, from 2017 to 2018. In 2017, he obtained his PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from UNSW, under the supervision of [Prof. Xuemin Lin (林学民)](https://www.cse.unsw.edu.au/~lxue/), with [Prof. Lu Qin (秦璐)](https://profiles.uts.edu.au/Lu.Qin) as co-supervisor. Prior to that, he got his master's/bachelor’s degree from Sichuan University in 2010/2013, both in Computer Science. 

Long's research interests lie in intelligent big data analytics, especially for the graph/network data.
